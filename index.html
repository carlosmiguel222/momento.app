Moment.o
Share Your Story, Live the Moment

Moment.o is a social media platform designed for sharing moments through stories. Unlike traditional platforms, Moment.o focuses solely on 24-hour stories, allowing users to post daily updates and react to others' stories. With a simple and engaging experience, users can share their moments effortlessly before they disappear, making every story special.
